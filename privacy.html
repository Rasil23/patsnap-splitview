Privacy Policy - Patsnap SplitView

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Patsnap SplitView is a Chrome extension that automatically arranges Patsnap patent pages side-by-side with search results.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The extension does not collect, transmit, or share any information about you or your browsing activity.

Local Storage

The extension saves one preference to your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API: your chosen mode (SplitView, Single Tab, or Off). This value never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions solely to perform its core function: tabs (to detect new Patsnap tabs), activeTab (to read the URL), storage (to save your mode preference), system.display (to detect screen size for snapping). None of this data is transmitted remotely.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. The extension has no analytics, no tracking, and no external network requests.
